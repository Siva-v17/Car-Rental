Who We Are

More Than Just a Rental

Founded in Salem in 2010, Stackly began with a simple belief: renting a car should feel as extraordinary as driving one. We set out to redefine what premium car hire could be — and never looked back.

  • Handpicked fleet of 120+ luxury, sports and executive vehicles
  • Serving over 4,800 satisfied customers across 35 locations
  • White-glove delivery and collection — airport, hotel or doorstep
  • 24/7 dedicated support and full roadside assistance worldwide
  • Multiple industry awards for service excellence

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

2012

Drivelix is Born

Founded in Manhattan with just 8 vehicles and an obsession with delivering the perfect driving experience.

2014

West Coast Expansion

Opened our Los Angeles and San Francisco locations, adding 25 new vehicles and 6 corporate partners.

2016

Digital Transformation

Launched our instant booking platform and mobile app — reducing reservation time from 20 minutes to under 60 seconds.

Reached a landmark in customer satisfaction, with a 4.97/5 average rating across all platforms.

Green Fleet Initiative

Global Partnership Network
