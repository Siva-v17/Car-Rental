FAQ

Common Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car? +
You'll need a valid driving licence (held for at least 2 years), a government-issued photo ID, and the credit/debit card used for booking. International drivers also need an IDP.
Is there a minimum age requirement? +
Yes — drivers must be at least 25 years old for our full luxury fleet. Drivers aged 21–24 may rent from our Essential range with a young driver surcharge of $35/day.
Can I pick up at the airport? +
Absolutely. We offer meet-and-greet at Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai and 30+ other locations across South India. Premium and Prestige plan holders enjoy this as a complimentary service.
What is your cancellation policy? +
Free cancellation up to 48 hours before your pickup time. Cancellations within 48 hours incur a one-day rental fee. No-shows are charged in full.
Is fuel included in the rental price? +
Vehicles are provided with a full tank and should be returned full. Alternatively, opt for our Pre-paid Fuel add-on and return the car empty at no extra charge.
Do you offer long-term rentals? +
Yes — monthly rentals from 28 days are available with significant discounts. All-inclusive packages covering insurance, servicing and vehicle swaps are available on request.
Can I add a second driver? +
Yes, additional drivers can be added to any booking for ₹499/day. They must meet the same age and licence requirements as the primary driver and be present at pickup.
What happens if the car breaks down? +
Call our 24/7 emergency line and we'll dispatch roadside assistance within 60 minutes. If the vehicle can't be repaired on-site, we'll deliver a replacement car to your location free of charge.
